About VMX


At VMX, we create powerful, efficient open-source software designed to simplify server management. Our products and services help you build secure, cost-effective, and scalable IT infrastructures based on open-source technologies. Development & Services With VMX technology, you get comprehensive yet user-friendly software solutions tailored to your needs. We understand that business continuity is critical, which is why we also offer technical assistance and enterprise-grade services to keep your operations running smoothly.

Every day, we work closely with our users to understand challenges, improve our products, and deliver better experiences. Backed by a global partner network and an active open-source community, VMX ensures efficient workflows for organizations of all sizes and sectors—including businesses, nonprofits, and educational institutions.

Our Story


VMX Founded in 2025 by Muhammed Asif and Jaimin Patel, VMX Server Solutions GmbH set out to develop intuitive GNU/Linux software for secure and scalable IT environments.
